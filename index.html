Plinky — Non-Custodial Creator Payments

My attempt to bring the ease of crypto payments to normal people on the internet, without the scary jargon, seed-phrase rituals, and general complexity of DeFi. A creator signs in with a passkey (no email, no password, no "web3" learning curve) and a non-custodial wallet is created silently in the background, then they share a link like plinky.to/you. Tippers pay USDC on Base straight from their own wallet into the creator's, so the app is never in the flow of funds: no custody, no payout step, and a "balance" is just a live on-chain balance read directly. The whole point is that it should feel like a normal tip jar, with the crypto hidden underneath.

Next.js 14 · TypeScript · Privy (embedded wallets) · wagmi / viem · Base · Supabase
